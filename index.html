mpstk - the Multiparty Session Types toolKit

mpstk is a toolkit for specifying multiparty protocols and verifying their properties. It is based on the new, generalised theory of multiparty session types presented in the companion paper:

Alceste Scalas and Nobuko Yoshida. Less is More: Multiparty Session Types Revisited. POPL 2019.
